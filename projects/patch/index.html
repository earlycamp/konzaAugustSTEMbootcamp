about lack of water

Lack of water has been a problem to the society and our country at large.This problem has brought about loss of lives leading to economy imbalance.With this challenge it will be quite impossible for our mother land to reach its vision[2030].

preservation of water catchment areas

presaervation of water catchment areas will play a vital role in the solution of the proble m.This is manifested by not cutting down trees near water sources.

digging of boreholes

With the help of government revenue people should be encouraged to dig boreholes in dry areas.

buying tanks

residents in the affected areas should engage in fund raising groups to buy water tanks.this water tanks will enable the people in harvesting rain water

educating people

last but not least people should be educated on the importance of preserving water.This is going to be light to guide them in thier daily activities.
