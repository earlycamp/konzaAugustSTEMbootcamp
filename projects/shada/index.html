About youth

Youth are faced by many problems.Some of them include lack of employment.It is a problem facing many of the youths in kenya.

solution 1

Creating co-curriculum activities which the youth may specialise in leading to careers.

solution 2

Creating more technical institutions which may absorb high school dropouts and those unable to qualify to university.

solution 3

Conducting seminars and training sessions of some activities to the youth eg web design.

Solution 4

Government creating job opportunities which the youth are good at eg entertainment.
